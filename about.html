17 Years of Resistance

Founded more than seventeen years ago, ACID RESISTANCE was born in Colombia with a clear mission: to promote and encourage the pure, uncompromising spirit of Acid Techno.

From underground parties in Bogotá to international showcases, the collective has held events around the world, leaving its mark in cities like La Plata, Buenos Aires, Curitiba, Sao Paulo, Vienna, Valencia, Berlin, and beyond. We have worked alongside the most legendary DJs and crews of the global scene, sharing the true warehouse energy with the world.

Now established in Barcelona for more than 4 years, Acid Resistance has gathered a new wave of local and international talent. Under the concept of the "Barcelombia Army On Acid", we are retaking the scene to bring back the golden era of what used to be known as Barcelona Acid City.

This is not just a label; it's a movement. Pure Acid. No compromises.
